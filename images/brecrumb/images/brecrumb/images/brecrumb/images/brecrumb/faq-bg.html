FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Some commonly asked questions about MyKhana, from our services to our cusines.

What types of events do you cater to?

We at MyKhana offer corporate event catering, cocktail/party catering, anniversary catering, birthday catering, baby shower catering among other events as well. Feel free to contact us for your event catering needs.

Can the menu be customized?

Be it small event catering, or corporate catering, the menu can be customized according to your preference. You can tell us what you require in the menu for your event catering.

Does MyKhana offer food tastings?

Yes, we at MyKhana do offer food tastings. Please make sure to tell your food tasting requests in advance.

Why is good catering service important for an event?

No matter if it is corporate event catering, party catering, anniversary catering that you need, we at MyKhana make sure that your event has delicious food that does not compromise on taste and quality. Catering services help you by providing a wide range of cuisines, and food catering services for your event.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions and special requests?

Yes, we can accommodate any specific requirements and needs for your event catering. You can tell us any request you have, and we will make sure to include that in the catering service provided by us.

How do you ensure food safety?

We make sure to maintain safety while preparing food. Our commitment to food safety is evident through rigorous measures that meet both FSSAI and WHO standards

What do catering services provide in a corporate event?

When you choose MyKhana for your corporate event catering, you services from catering services, staff to serve and customized menus to choose from. We offer food that is best in taste and quality.

What are the cuisines that you offer?

We at MyKhana offer cuisines like North Indian, South India, Chinese among others. You can contact us to get the best food catering service for your event.
