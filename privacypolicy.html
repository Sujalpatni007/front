Privacy Policy - Isha Group Caterers

Isha Group Caterers is committed to respecting the privacy of every person who accesses our website www.ishagroupcaterers.com. Your privacy is important to us, and we strive to take reasonable care in protecting the information we receive from you.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and process your data. By accessing our website or using our services, you agree to the terms of this policy. Please read carefully to understand our practices regarding personal data.

By using our website and providing information, you consent to our data collection and usage as described herein. We reserve the right to update this policy, and continued use of our website constitutes acceptance of any changes.

INFORMATION COLLECTION AND USE

When you interact with our website, we may collect personal information including:

  • Name, contact number, email address
  • Billing and delivery addresses
  • Event details and catering requirements
  • Payment information for service bookings

We may use this information to:

  • Process and fulfill catering service requests
  • Communicate order updates and confirmations
  • Provide customer support
  • Send promotional offers and newsletters (with consent)
  • Improve our services and website experience

INFORMATION SHARING

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers necessary for order fulfillment
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Our affiliated business entities

COOKIES AND TRACKING

We use cookies to enhance user experience, analyze site traffic, and personalize content. You can modify browser settings to decline cookies, though this may limit website functionality.

SECURITY

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

CONTACT INFORMATION

For privacy-related queries, contact us at: privacy@ishagroupcaterers.com
